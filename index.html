Charbroiled salmon

Here's a very simple salmon reciepe that starts with a marinade of soy sauce, red wine,ginger and black pepper. grill over hot coals for about 5 minutes per side,basking frequently with extra marinade.

Prep Time: Cook Time: Additional Time:
5 mins 10 mins 2 hours
Total Time: Serving: Yield:
2hrs 15mins 4 4 Serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons red wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds salmon steaks
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley,for garnish
  • 4 slice lemons for garnish
Directions

Step 1

Combine soy sauce,red wine,ginger and black pepper in a large,resealable plastic bad. Seal,and shake vigorously to mix Ingredients. Add salmon steaks,squeeze out excess air,and seal. Refrigerate,turning frequentlyto keep all sides in contact with the liquid,for no less than 2 hours.

Step 2

Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat.

Step 3

Cook on ahot grill for about 5 minutes per side,basting freely with extra marinade. Serve with parsley garnish and lemon slices
